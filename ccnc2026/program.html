Workshop Program

6G-SAGA: Intelligent Aerial and Spaceborne Systems for 6G – Communication, Sensing, and Autonomy

In conjunction with IEEE CCNC 2026

Planned Format

The workshop is structured as a half-day (4-hour) event:

  • Opening Remarks – 5 minutes
  • Keynote Talk – 45 minutes (Invited Speaker)
  • Three Technical Paper Presentations – 25 minutes each
  • Break – 30 minutes
  • Optional Second Invited Talk – 45 minutes
  • Three Additional Technical Presentations – 25 minutes each
  • Closing Remarks – 5 minutes

Invited Technical Talks

  • Speaker 1: Dr. Satoshi Konishi (President and CEO, KDDI Research Inc., Japan)
  • Speaker 2: Prof. Tomoaki Ohtsuki (Keio University, Japan)
