6G-SAGA Workshop

The 1st International Workshop on Intelligent Aerial and Spaceborne Systems for 6G: Communication, Sensing, and Autonomy

In conjunction with IEEE CCNC 2026

Overview

The 6G-SAGA workshop focuses on integrating intelligent aerial and spaceborne systems—such as satellites, UAVs, and HAPS—into the architecture of 6G networks. It emphasizes AI-native design, joint communication and sensing, and autonomous operations. This workshop explores the evolution of Satellite–Aerial–Ground Integrated Networks (SAGIN) into adaptive, secure, and scalable infrastructures that extend connectivity and intelligence from the sky and beyond.

Key Themes

  • AI-native architectures for SAGIN and Beyond-SAGIN systems
  • Joint communication, computing, and sensing (3CS)
  • Edge intelligence and federated learning
  • UAV/HAPS-assisted dynamic network slicing
  • Security and privacy in aerial and space networks
